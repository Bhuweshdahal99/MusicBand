ABOUT ME

The story behind the ethereal sounds and digital dreams

Early Life


Full Name: Alan Olav Walker


Born: August 24, 1997, in Northampton, England


Raised in: Bergen, Norway


Background: He has a British father and a Norwegian mother. Despite not having formal music training, Alan was always interested in computers, programming, and graphic design.


Start in Music

In his teens, Walker taught himself music production by watching YouTube tutorials and experimenting with software like FL Studio.


He was inspired by artists like Hans Zimmer, Ahrix, and David Whistle.

Breakthrough with "Fade"


In 2014, Alan released the instrumental track "Fade" on YouTube and SoundCloud.


The song quickly gained popularity in the EDM community due to its melodic and emotional style.


"Fade" later evolved into "Faded" with vocals by Iselin Solheim, released in 2015.



Massive Global Success


"Faded" became a global hit: Over 3.7 billion views on YouTube Certified multi-platinum in over 10 countries Became an anthem for his signature sound: atmospheric, emotional EDM
